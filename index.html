Company Overview

We are a leading
global systems
integrator and
managed
services provider.

Established in 2008 as Business Connexion ICT Services Nig Ltd by Business Connexion PTY (BCX), a
South African based information and communications technology company with a wide footprint of
operations in Africa, Europe and the Middle East and listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange,
Arravo Ltd is a leading global systems integrator and managed services provider.

In 2018 when the BCX Group took a business decision to divest its stake outside Southern Africa, the
local management in conjunction with a group of investors acquired the company, instituted a new
Board of Directors and in 2019, the new brand name Arravo was launched.

With over 300 of the finest ICT minds in Nigeria, we spearhead
