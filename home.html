Fabrication

At ServerCrib Technology Limited, we specialize in the fabrication and installation of various types of pipework and steelwork, ensuring excellent finishing tailored to our customers' needs and technical specifications.

Pipe Work:
 Our team at ServerCrib Technology Limited takes pride in being recognized as leaders in pipe work and fabrication. With our highly skilled welders, we confidently undertake various welding jobs including TIG, MIG, ARC, and OXY-ACETYLENE. Our commitment to quality ensures that each weld is meticulously executed, leaving behind no traces of leaks and guaranteeing aesthetic appeal.
Equipment Installation: We excel in the installation of machinery and equipment, handling each project with care and precision to meet our clients' expectations.

Thermal Insulation and Cladding:
In addition to fabrication and installation, our engineering expertise extends to insulation solutions such as thermal insulation for pipes and tanks, ensuring optimal performance and energy efficiency.

Electrical Installation:
ServerCrib Technology Limited is renowned for delivering top-notch electrical installation services, solidifying our position as a premier provider of industrial engineering solutions.

Industrial Fittings & Consumable Supply:
We offer a wide range of stainless and mild steel fittings and consumables, including pipes, elbows, flanges, valves, bolts, nuts, washers, and more, ensuring our clients have access to high-quality components for their projects.

Electrodes:
We supply a variety of electrodes, including mild steel, stainless, low hydrogen, shield arc, and fleet electrodes, meeting the diverse needs of our customers.

Insulation - Cladding & Lagging:
Our expertise extends to insulation solutions such as cladding and lagging, providing effective thermal insulation to enhance safety and performance.

Pressure Testing:
We conduct thorough pressure testing to ensure the integrity and reliability of our installations, delivering peace of mind to our clients.
