About Servercrib

ICT,IoT & AI Service Provider

Established in 2008 as Business Connexion ICT Services Nig Ltd by
Business Connexion PTY (BCX), a South African based information and
communications technology company with a wide footprint of operations in Africa,
Europe and the Middle East and also listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange,
Arravo Ltd is a leading global systems integrator and managed services provider.

In 2018 when the BCX Group took a business decision to divest its stake outside
Southern Africa, the local management in conjunction with a group of investors
acquired the company, instituted a new Board of Directors and in 2020, the new brand
name Arravo was launched.
